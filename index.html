About Us


Our Vision

To build a community where the vulnerable and the marginalized would have access to education, free from poverty and have right to social development.

Our Mission

To develop children into productive citizenship through knowledge and capacity buildings for sustainment and serve humanity in equality, equity and harmony for happier community.

Aims and Objectives

1.
To prevent and relief poverty for humanity
2.
To build a better future for the children and youths.
3.
To promote education for the indigents and the intelligent students.
4.
To promote capacity building through the workshops, seminars and conferences nationally and internationally.
5.
To advocate for social change and transformation of lives of Nigerians particularly women and children.
